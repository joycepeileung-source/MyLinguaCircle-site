Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry, application, or payment review form, MyLinguaCircle may collect your name, email, country, time zone, language goals, current ability, preferred program, and messages you choose to share with us.

How We Use Information

We use your information to answer questions, recommend a suitable language circle, manage enrollment, coordinate class communication, provide payment support, and improve the learning experience.

Payments

Card and payment details should be entered only through the secure checkout pages provided by Stripe or PayPal. MyLinguaCircle does not store full card numbers on this website.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary details with trusted service providers who help us operate forms, payments, scheduling, email, and class delivery.

Your Choices

You may ask us to update, correct, or delete your information by contacting support@mylinguacircle.com. Some records may be retained when needed for enrollment, payment, legal, or security purposes.
