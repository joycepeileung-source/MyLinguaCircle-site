Cookie Policy

What Cookies Are

Cookies and similar storage technologies help a website remember information in your browser. They can support basic site features, form behavior, analytics, and secure third-party checkout experiences.

How We Use Them

MyLinguaCircle may use necessary cookies or browser storage to keep forms working, remember enrollment details during checkout review, understand site performance, and connect you with payment or form providers.

Third-Party Services

Services such as form processors, Stripe, PayPal, Google-hosted fonts, and analytics tools may set their own cookies or collect technical information according to their own policies.

Your Choices

You can block, delete, or manage cookies through your browser settings. Some site functions, form submissions, or secure checkout steps may not work as expected if certain cookies are disabled.

Contact

Questions about cookies can be sent to support@mylinguacircle.com.
