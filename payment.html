Enrollment Terms

Cancellation, refund, and transfer policy

We want every student to be placed in a cohort that fits their level, goals, and schedule.

Full refund if we cannot place you: If MyLinguaCircle is unable to place you in a suitable cohort, you will receive a full refund.

24-hour cancellation window: After payment, you may cancel your enrollment within 24 hours for a full refund.

After 24 hours: After the 24-hour cancellation window, tuition is non-refundable unless MyLinguaCircle is unable to provide a suitable cohort placement. If your schedule changes before the cohort begins, you may request to transfer your enrollment to a future cohort, subject to availability.

After the cohort begins: Once your cohort has started, tuition is non-refundable. If you are unable to continue, your remaining tuition value may be transferred to a future cohort or applied toward eligible Language Club sessions at the current rate of $49 USD per session.

Language Club application: Any remaining tuition value applied toward Language Club sessions is subject to availability, is calculated at $49 USD per session, and must be used within 12 months of the original payment date.

Privacy: Your application details are used only to process enrollment, cohort placement, payment support, and class communication.
