Terms of Service

Program Enrollment

By submitting an application or payment, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and that you are enrolling in the selected MyLinguaCircle program for personal learning purposes.

Payment and Access

Program access is confirmed after payment is completed through the checkout method provided. Pricing, schedules, cohort availability, and program details may change before enrollment is finalized.

Payment and Refund Policy

All MyLinguaCircle program fees are listed in USD and are processed through third-party payment providers such as PayPal. By submitting payment, you are reserving a seat in a limited-size cohort.

Payment Processing Fees

Payment processing fees charged by PayPal or any other third-party payment provider are non-refundable. If a refund is approved, the refunded amount may be reduced by any non-refundable payment processing fees, currency conversion costs, or transaction costs charged by the payment provider.

Refund Eligibility

Refund requests must be submitted in writing to support@mylinguacircle.com.

If you request a refund at least 7 days before the cohort start date, you may receive a refund minus non-refundable payment processing fees.

If you request a refund within 7 days of the cohort start date, you may be eligible for a 50% refund minus non-refundable payment processing fees.

Refund requests made within 48 hours of the cohort start date are not eligible for a cash refund, but you may request a one-time transfer to a future cohort, subject to availability.

Once a cohort has started, program fees are non-refundable.

Missed Classes

Missed classes, late arrivals, scheduling conflicts, technical issues on the student's side, or changes in personal availability do not qualify for refunds. When possible, MyLinguaCircle may provide class materials, practice suggestions, or other learning support, but this does not guarantee a refund or makeup class.

Cohort Transfers

Students may request one transfer to a future cohort if the request is made before the cohort begins. Transfers are subject to availability and approval by MyLinguaCircle. A cohort transfer has no cash value and cannot be exchanged for a refund after the original refund period has passed.

Cancellations by MyLinguaCircle

If MyLinguaCircle cancels a cohort or is unable to provide the program you paid for, you may choose either a full refund or a transfer to a future cohort.

Currency Conversion and Bank Fees

MyLinguaCircle is not responsible for currency conversion fees, foreign transaction fees, bank fees, PayPal fees, or other charges imposed by the student's bank, card issuer, or payment provider.

Disputes and Chargebacks

If you have a question about a payment or refund, please contact us first at support@mylinguacircle.com so we can help resolve the issue. In the event of a PayPal dispute, bank reversal, or credit card chargeback, MyLinguaCircle may provide enrollment records, payment records, attendance records, course access records, and communication history as evidence that the service was provided or reserved.

Participation

Students are expected to participate respectfully, arrive prepared, and avoid sharing private class materials, meeting links, recordings, or community spaces with people outside the enrolled cohort.

Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent to support@mylinguacircle.com.
